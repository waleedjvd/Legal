Return & Exchange Policies

Return Policy

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 30 days of the purchase date for a full refund or exchange. All returned items must be in their original condition, unused, and with all original packaging and tags attached.

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team at lrcustomers@gmail.com with your order number and the reason for the return. We will provide you with a return authorization number and instructions on how to proceed with the return.

Please note that return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item received was damaged or incorrect.

Exchange Policy

If you would like to exchange an item for a different size, color, or style, please contact our customer service team at lrcustomers@gmail.com with your order number and the details of the exchange request.

Exchanges must be requested within 30 days of the purchase date. The item being exchanged must be in its original condition, unused, and with all original packaging and tags attached.

Once we receive the item to be exchanged, we will process your request and ship the new item to you. Please note that the customer is responsible for any shipping costs associated with returning the original item and receiving the new item.
